INSTRUCTIONS

  1. The aim of the game is to more points than the opponent.
  2. The active player will roll the die and a random number from 1 to 6 will appear on the die, which will be treated as your current score.
  3. The current score will add up, depending upon how many times you roll.
  4. On clicking on 'Hold' button, your summed up current score will be added to your total score, and your turn will end.
  5. But if a player rolls a '1' on the die, the player turn ends, loosing all the accumulated points. Hence, '1' means Danger!.
  6. The player with the maximum points at the end of the game will be declared 'Winner'.

Time Trial

  1. In this game mode, apart of each other, you also have to race against time.
  2. The Time Limit can be set in the begining of the game.

Beat The Score

  1. In this game mode, players are required to acquire to get a minimum preset score before the opponent to win.

Rounds

  1. In this game mode, the players have predecided number of turns/rounds.
  2. Players need to get more points than the opponent in the set number of rounds to win
  3. The players can only roll max 3 times in a singlr round.
  4. In this game mode rolling a '1' does not result in loosing all the accumulated points. '1' is a safe number.
  5. But trying to roll more than 3 times will result in an automatic change of turns.
